Refund Policy

Clear and fair refund guidelines.

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

1. Overview

At Radhe Enterprises, we strive to ensure our customers are satisfied with our services. However, we understand that circumstances may change. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be granted for our various services.

2. Driving School Refunds

  • Full Refund: If a student cancels their enrollment at least 48 hours before the first scheduled lesson, a full refund (minus a small administrative fee) will be issued.
  • Partial Refund: If a student discontinues training after lessons have begun, a refund will be calculated on a pro-rata basis for the remaining unused lessons, minus a cancellation fee.
  • No Refund: No refunds will be issued for lessons missed without prior notice (24 hours) or after the training course validity period has expired.

3. Tour & Travel Refunds

Refunds for tour packages are subject to the cancellation policies of our partner hotels and transport providers:

  • Cancellation 30+ days before departure: 90% refund of the booking amount.
  • Cancellation 15-29 days before departure: 50% refund of the booking amount.
  • Cancellation 7-14 days before departure: 25% refund of the booking amount.
  • Cancellation less than 7 days before departure: No refund.
  • Note: Visa fees and other non-refundable administrative costs are excluded from refunds.

4. Car Rental Refunds

  • Advance Booking: Cancellations made 24 hours prior to the pickup time are eligible for a full refund of the booking advance.
  • Late Cancellation: Cancellations made within 24 hours of pickup may incur a cancellation charge equivalent to one day's rental.
  • Early Return: If a vehicle is returned earlier than the scheduled date, refunds for the unused days will be at the discretion of management and subject to minimum rental period terms.

5. License Consultancy Refunds

  • Service Not Initiated: Full refund if you cancel before we have started processing your application or booked an appointment.
  • Application Rejected: If an application is rejected due to an error on our part, we will re-apply at no extra cost or provide a refund of our service fee.
  • Government Fees: Government fees paid to the RTO are non-refundable once the payment is processed on the official portal.
  • Client Error: No refund if rejection is due to incorrect documents provided by the client or failure to attend the RTO appointment.

6. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact us via email or phone with your booking details and reason for cancellation. Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days and credited back to the original method of payment.
